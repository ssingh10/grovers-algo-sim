How Grover's Works
01
Superposition
Apply Hadamard to all qubits → equal probability across all 2ⁿ states.
02
Oracle
Flip the phase of the target state. Probabilities unchanged — information encoded in phase.
03
Diffusion
Reflect amplitudes about their mean (inversion about average). Target amplitude grows each iteration.
04
Measure
After O(√N) iterations, the target state has near-100% probability. Classical: O(N).

How Shor's Works
01
Pick Base a
Choose random a coprime to N. If gcd(a,N)≠1, you've already found a factor!
02
Quantum Period Finding
QFT finds the period r of f(x) = aˣ mod N — exponentially faster than classical.
03
Extract Factors
If r is even: compute gcd(aʳ/² ± 1, N) to get non-trivial factors.
04
Speedup
Classical: O(e^(n^1/3)). Quantum: O(n³). Breaks RSA encryption at scale.
